The King's Hand

Your Ultimate Clash Royale Companion

Automate your Clash Royale Pass

The King's Hand is a powerful bot designed to effortlessly farm your Clash Royale pass by playing games for you, letting you focus on the fun parts of the game.

Join our Discord Server View on GitHub

Key Features

Auto-Farming

Plays games for you, earning crowns, chests, and trophies to advance your pass.

Flexible Modes

Supports both 1v1 and 2v2 modes, adapting to your preferred style of play.

Intelligent System

The bot's intelligent system is a work in progress. For now, it places cards at random. The logic is not designed to win games but to play a high volume of games, which eventually leads to earning crowns and progressing your pass.

About the Project

The King's Hand is a passion project developed and maintained by a solo developer. I built this bot to help players who don't have enough time to grind the game, ensuring everyone can enjoy the rewards of the Clash Royale Pass. I'm committed to its continuous improvement and providing a valuable tool for the community.
